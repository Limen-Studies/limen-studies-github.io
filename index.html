ABOUT THE QUESTION?

National Grid is currently constructing extensive and invasive gas pipeline infrastructure in downstate NY. Their case for the necessity of this infrastructure is based on an internal Long-Term Capacity Report. Synapse Energy Economics conducted an assesment of this report and found that not only are National Grid’s findings flawed - but - incorporating missing and miscalculated data concludes that a supply gap does not exist. Furthermore, there is excess capacity within the existing infrastructure. This contrast questions Nationals Grid’s predictions and motivations.
